Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy outlines the privacy practices for MBralway.com and pertains exclusively to information collected through this website. It informs you about the following: the personally identifiable information collected from you via the website, how it is utilized, and with whom it may be shared. It also details your available options regarding the use of your data, the security measures implemented to prevent misuse of your information, and how you can correct any inaccuracies in your information.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We are the sole proprietors of the information collected on this site. We only access or collect information that you voluntarily provide to us via email or other direct communications. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone. We will use your information to respond to you regarding the reason you contacted us. We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, except as necessary to fulfill your request, such as making a booking or providing information on Rail Explorers. Unless you request otherwise, we may contact you via email in the future to inform you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy police.

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You can opt out of future communications from us at any time. You may contact us via the email address or phone number provided on our website to:

  • Check what data we have about you, if any.
  • Update or correct any data we have about you.
  • Request the deletion of any data we have about you.
  • Raise any concerns you have regarding our use of your data.

Security

We take precautions to safeguard your information. When you submit sensitive data via the website, it is protected both online and offline. Any sensitive information (such as credit card details) that we collect is encrypted and securely transmitted to us. You can verify the security by looking for a closed lock icon on your web browser or "https" at the beginning of the webpage address. In addition to using encryption for online transmissions, we also protect your information offline. Access to personally identifiable information is restricted to employees who need it to perform specific tasks (e.g., billing or customer service). The computers and servers storing this information are maintained in a secure environment.

Updates

Our Privacy Policy may be updated periodically, and all changes will be posted on this page. If you believe we are not adhering to this privacy policy, please contact us immediately via email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via emails.
