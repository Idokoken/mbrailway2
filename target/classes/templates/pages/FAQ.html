Some of the frequently asked questions from our customers are outlined below

1. What is MBrailway?

MBrailway is an online train reservation platform that allows users to book train tickets easily and securely

2. How do I contact MBrailway customer support?

You can contact us through our website, email - support@mbrailway.com, or phone number - +234703558877.

3. What are the benefits of creating an account on MBrailway?

Creating an account allows you to save your booking history, track your orders, and receive exclusive offers.

4. How can I book a train ticket?

You can book a train ticket by searching for your preferred route, selecting the travel date, and choosing your seat type. Once you've chosen, proceed to payment to confirm your booking.

5. Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking by logging into your account and accessing the "My Bookings" section. Please note that cancellation charges may apply depending on how close to the travel date you cancel

6. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, digital wallets, and UPI

7. How will I receive my ticket after booking?

Once your booking is confirmed, your e-ticket will be sent to your registered email address and mobile number. You can also view and download your ticket by logging into your account.

8. What should I do if I miss my train?

If you miss your train, you will not be eligible for a refund. We recommend reaching the station well in advance of the departure time. In case of emergencies, you can check with our customer service for further assistance.

10. Can I reserve a seat for someone else?

Yes, you can book tickets for other passengers by providing their correct details during the booking process.

11. How do I check my train's schedule and status?

You can check the current schedule and real-time status of your train on the "Train Schedule" or "Track Train" section of our website. Simply enter your train number or route to get live updates.

12. Do you offer discounts for children, seniors, or group bookings?

Yes, we offer discounts for children, senior citizens, and group bookings. Please check the discounts section while booking or contact our customer support for more details.

13. What happens if my train is delayed or canceled?

In case of a delay or cancellation, you will be notified via SMS or email. You may choose to reschedule your trip or apply for a refund based on our cancellation policies

14. How do I contact customer support for more help?

You can contact our customer support team via phone or email. Visit the "Contact Us" page for more information
